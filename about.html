About the course

We are excited to run a new PGT course that has young people, their families and the community at its centre; MSc Applied Developmental Psychology. It has a number of unique features including a weekly Community of Practice bringing interdisciplinary researchers, policy-makers and practitioners together. Through which students will gain a first hand understanding of the translational nature of developmental science to some of the main challenges for young people and their families. There is also the opportunity for a placement to work on an intervention with a partner organisation.

Please contact us if you would like to know more. In addition, if you know of anyone who this would be a good fit for (i.e., has studied Psychology, or related discipline; 2:2 and above) to prepare them for further study and employment we would be grateful if you would circulate the opportunity.
