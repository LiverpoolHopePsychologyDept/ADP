Overview

We are excited to run a new Postgraduate Taught course that has young people, their families and the community at its centre; MSc Applied Developmental Psychology. It has a number of unique features including a weekly Community of Practice bringing interdisciplinary researchers, policy-makers and practitioners together. Through which students will gain a first hand understanding of the translational nature of developmental science to some of the main challenges for young people and their families. There is also the opportunity for a placement to work on an intervention with a partner organisation.

More information is available here: About the course & curriculum
